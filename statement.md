# **PROBLEM STATEMENT**
Students often struggle to manage their tasks, meet deadlines, and track the time they spend studying. Without a structured system, it becomes easy to forget important tasks, deadlines or lose track of study hours. **Smart Study Planner** aims to solve this problem by providing an easy to use tool to organize tasks and monitor study progress.

# **SCOPE OF PROJECT**
- Manage and organize daily tasks with deadlines
- Provide a clear picture of completed and pending tasks
- Track study hours for each day
- Help students monitor their study habbits

# **TARGET USERS**
- School and college students
- Anyone who wants to improve time management and track their study hours

# **HIGH-LEVEL FEATURES**

1.Task Management


2.Assign deadlines for tasks


3.Study Hours Tracking


4.Persistent Data Storage


5.User Friendly Menu
